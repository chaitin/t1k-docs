Designed for NGINX

Leveraging NGINX's strong presence in the proxy market, Chaitin Tech has entered the competitive WAF market by developing the T1K module since its establishment in 2014, earning customer trust.

The solution based on the T1K module supports comprehensive request and response monitoring for widely used proxies in the industry such as NGINX OSS, NGINX Plus, OpenResty, APISIX, Kong, and Ingress Nginx, through dynamic loading.

Moreover, functionalities such as automatic bypass and high-load degradation ensure business continuity, while BOT/API protection and other security measures provide comprehensive network security for enterprises.
